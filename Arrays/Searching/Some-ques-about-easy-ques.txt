1️. First vs Last occurrence me direction kya change hota hai?

First occurrence → high = mid - 1 (LEFT jaate hain)

Last occurrence → low = mid + 1 (RIGHT jaate hain)

 Reason:
Answer milne ke baad bhi better index dhoondhna hota hai

first = aur left me?

last = aur right me?
