## CLASSIC BINARY SEARCH (BASE LAYER)
### Core intuition

Har step pe decide karo:
“Answer left half me hai ya right half me?”

Example
arr = [1,3,5,7,9], target = 7


mid = 5 → chhota → right

mid = 7 → mil gaya 

Important:
Binary search comparison nahi, decision hai.
